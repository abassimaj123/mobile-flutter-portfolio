SUBJECT: FOIL Request – Parking Meter Locations and Street Parking Rules

This is a request under New York's Freedom of Information Law (FOIL) for the following records:

1. PARKING METER INVENTORY
   Complete dataset of all NYC DOT-regulated parking meters with:
   - Meter ID / sensor ID
   - Street address (block number, lot number)
   - Latitude and longitude coordinates
   - Parking rate ($X.XX per hour)
   - Operating hours (e.g., 8 AM - 10 PM, Mon-Fri)
   - Maximum stay duration (in minutes or hours)
   - Days of operation (e.g., Mon-Fri, Mon-Sun)
   - Effective date of current rates/rules

   Requested format: CSV, Excel (.xlsx), or JSON (machine-readable preferred)

2. STREET PARKING REGULATIONS (Curb Regulations)
   Complete database of all NYC DOT street parking regulations by street segment:
   - Street name
   - From cross street
   - To cross street
   - Side of street (north, south, east, west)
   - Regulation type code (e.g., NO PARKING, PARKING 2-4PM, METER)
   - Full regulation text (days and hours applicable)
   - Effective date

   Requested format: CSV, Excel (.xlsx), or JSON

3. PARKING SIGN INVENTORY (Optional but valuable)
   If available, a database of parking signs with:
   - Sign location (address, coordinates)
   - Regulation code
   - Sign type classification
   - Installation date

   Requested format: CSV, Excel, or JSON

JUSTIFICATION FOR REQUEST:
This data will be used to develop a mobile application ("ParkSmart") that helps NYC residents and visitors understand parking rules and avoid violations. The app will increase public understanding of parking regulations and reduce unintentional violations, benefiting both drivers and the city's revenue.

DATA WILL BE PROVIDED AT NO COST:
The resulting parking app will be provided free to the public and will direct users to NYC Department of Transportation official resources.
